About battle_length_std.

Open Scope nat_scope.


Theorem battle_length_std_Hardy (alpha : E0) :
  alpha ≠ E0zero →
  ∀ k , 1 ≤ k →
             ∃ l: nat,
               H'_ alpha k - k ≤ l ∧
               battle_length standard k (iota (cnf alpha)) l.
